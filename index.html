Ontology Quality Evaluation

Evaluate ontology fragments generated from a relational database schema. For each table, you will see four anonymised ontology fragments and score them on seven quality dimensions.

Before you begin

  • You will evaluate 4 tables (from two databases), each with 4 ontology fragments shown side-by-side.
  • Fragments are labelled A, B, C, D — method names are hidden.
  • For each table, score each fragment (0–5) on 7 quality dimensions, rank them 1st–4th, and optionally leave comments.
  • Estimated time: 10–15 minutes.
  • At the end, download your results as a JSON file and return it to the study coordinator.
